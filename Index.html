What About Yogyakarta?

The city of Yogyakarta is the capital and administrative center of the province of the Special Region of Yogyakarta, Indonesia. This city is a big city that maintains traditional concepts and Javanese culture. The city of Yogyakarta is the residence of Sultan Hamengkubuwana and the Duke of Paku Alam

What About Bromo?

Mount Bromo or in the Tenggerese spelled "Brama", also called the Tengger Caldera, is an active volcano in East Java, Indonesia.
